Street Food Festival



Join food enthusiasts from around the world at the annual Bangkok Street Food Festival, a culinary extravaganza that celebrates the vibrant street food culture of Thailand. Held in the bustling streets of Bangkok, this festival showcases the rich diversity of Thai cuisine through an array of mouthwatering dishes, lively performances, and interactive food experiences.

Street Food Stalls: Wander through the maze of street food stalls that line the festival grounds, offering an endless variety of Thai street food delights. From fragrant curries and grilled skewers to crispy spring rolls and sweet treats, there's something to satisfy every craving at the Bangkok Street Food Festival.

Regional Specialties: Discover the culinary treasures of Thailand's diverse regions as you sample authentic dishes from different parts of the country. Taste the fiery flavors of northeastern Isaan cuisine, indulge in southern Thai seafood delicacies, and savor the aromatic curries of central Thailand.

Cultural Performance: Experience the vibrant culture of Thailand through traditional dance performances, live music, and cultural displays. Marvel at the graceful movements of Thai dancers, groove to the rhythm of traditional music, and admire colorful exhibitions showcasing Thailand's rich heritage.


PizzaFest



PizzaFest is a vibrant and popular festival celebrating pizza. It typically features a variety of activities, including pizza tasting, cooking demonstrations, and live entertainment. Originated in Naples, Italy, the birthplace of pizza. Aims to honor and promote the rich history and tradition of pizza making.

Pizza Tasting: Attendees can sample a wide range of pizzas from traditional Margherita to innovative gourmet creations.

Cooking Demonstrations: Live demos by renowned chefs showcasing pizza-making techniques and recipes.

Competitions: Pizza-making contests where chefs compete for titles like Best Pizza or Most Creative Topping.

Workshops: Hands-on sessions teaching participants how to make their own pizzas, including dough preparation and baking tips.

While originating in Italy, PizzaFest has inspired similar events worldwide, adapting the festival to local cultures and tastes. PizzaFest is a celebration of one of the world's most beloved foods. It offers a fun, educational, and delicious experience for all attendees.


National Cherry Festival



The National Cherry Festival is an annual event celebrating cherries. Held in Traverse City, Michigan, which is known as the "Cherry Capital of the World." Originated in 1925 as the Blessing of the Blossoms Festival. Renamed the National Cherry Festival in 1931.Live music and performances from local and national artists. Carnival rides, games, and fireworks.Kid-friendly activities such as face painting, craft booths, and fun runs. Cherry-themed educational activities and interactive exhibits.

Cherry Tasting: Opportunities to sample various cherry-based products such as pies, jams, and juices.

Parades: Includes the Grand Royale Parade and the Junior Royale Parade, featuring floats, bands, and community groups.

Cherry Pit Spitting Contest: A fun and popular competition to see who can spit a cherry pit the farthest.

Cherry Pie Eating Contest: Contestants compete to eat the most cherry pies in a given time.

Air Show: Features thrilling performances by the U.S. Navy Blue Angels and other aerobatic teams.
