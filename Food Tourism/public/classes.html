THAI COOKING CLASSES -


Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Thai cuisine with hands-on cooking classes that offer a unique opportunity to learn the art of Thai cooking from expert chefs. Whether you're a novice cook or a seasoned culinary enthusiast, Thai cooking classes cater to all skill levels and provide an unforgettable culinary experience.

Authentic Recipes: Learn to prepare authentic Thai dishes using traditional recipes passed down through generations. From aromatic curries and spicy stir-fries to fragrant soups and delectable desserts, Thai cooking classes cover a wide range of dishes that showcase the diverse flavors of Thai cuisine.

Fresh Ingredients: Discover the importance of fresh ingredients in Thai cooking and explore the vibrant array of herbs, spices, and produce used in traditional Thai recipes. From lemongrass and galangal to Thai basil and kaffir lime leaves, each ingredient plays a crucial role in creating the distinctive flavors of Thai cuisine.

Hands-On Experience: Roll up your sleeves and get ready to dive into the culinary action as you participate in hands-on cooking demonstrations and practical exercises. Learn essential cooking techniques such as stir-frying, steaming, and curry paste making under the guidance of experienced Thai chefs.

Cultural Insights: Gain insights into the rich cultural heritage and culinary traditions of Thailand as you delve into the history and origins of iconic Thai dishes. Discover the influence of regional cuisines, royal Thai cuisine, and street food culture on the diverse culinary landscape of Thailand.

ITALIAN PASTA MAKING WORKSHOP -


Indulge in the time-honored tradition of pasta making with an immersive workshop that invites you to roll up your sleeves and discover the secrets of Italian cuisine. Led by expert chefs in the heart of Italy, pasta making workshops offer a hands-on experience that celebrates the artistry and craftsmanship behind one of Italy's most beloved culinary treasures.

Hands-On Instruction: Step into the kitchen and learn the art of pasta making from skilled Italian chefs who are passionate about preserving Italy's culinary heritage. Receive hands-on instruction on mixing, kneading, and shaping pasta dough to perfection, as you create an array of pasta shapes from scratch.

Quality Ingredients: Explore the importance of quality ingredients in Italian cuisine and discover the key components of authentic pasta dough, including flour, eggs, and water. Learn how to select the finest ingredients and master the art of balancing flavors to create pasta dough that is both tender and flavorful.

Traditional Techniques: Dive into the traditional techniques and time-tested methods used in Italian pasta making, from hand-rolling pasta dough to shaping intricate pasta shapes such as tagliatelle, fettuccine, and ravioli. Discover the significance of each pasta shape and its culinary versatility in Italian cooking.

Culinary Creativity: Unleash your culinary creativity as you experiment with different pasta shapes, fillings, and sauces to create unique and flavorful dishes. From classic combinations like spaghetti with marinara sauce to innovative creations like stuffed pasta with creamy ricotta filling, the possibilities are endless.
